Why you may need a consultant...

With the evolution of technology, storytellers are popping up everywhere. Still there is a proper format and technique required to set your project apart from the rest. If you have a great script and a budget in place, but have little or no background in filmmaking, an experienced consultant can get you through this very beneficial but demanding process. I have over 10 years experience in the business, a bachelors degree in film and television production, and a love for meeting fellow filmmakers. If you need help completing a film project, no matter what the format, look no further! I can walk you through pre-production, production, and the post production process. Consulting can be exercised personally on set, by phone, and or via video streaming. My fees are reasonable and consultation is free. If this sounds like what you need, please contact me via this post. I will return your email within 48 hrs.
